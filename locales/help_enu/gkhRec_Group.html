Group record

Groups make navigation easy; they group people by a common social sign.

For example, you can create "Participants in The Great Patriotic War" group with respective members. Or create "Correspondents" group for people you are currently staying in contact with.

To add new person to a group you can use any of the following method:

  • click "Add" button on the "Members" tab of group editing dialog;
  • click "Add" button on the "Groups" tab of group editing dialog.

Using "Remove" button from the same tabs, you can remove persons from groups.

There is a table on the "Groups" tab of the main work window, where you can find all groups stored in database. Each group record has a summary area to the right of the table, where you can view entire information about group and full list of members.
