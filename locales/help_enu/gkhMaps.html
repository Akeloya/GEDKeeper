Maps

GEDKeeper uses Google Maps (© Google) to show maps.

Using maps service GEDKeeper shows you locations of births, deaths and living, defined in database, right on a map. There two modes available: one shows births, deaths or living location for all persons in database; another one shows all locations of any event for selected person.

Before you will call the map service, you can filter persons out using general GEDKeeper filters.
