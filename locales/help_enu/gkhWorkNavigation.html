Navigation

One of the major GEDKeeper target is to provide the most efficient navigation.

That is why the main window of GEDKeeper allows quick access to six main category of records and has summary area to the right of the lists, where you can easily view information about a record and use hypertext links to go to another, related records.

The following record types are self-contained:

  • Notes - any text added to person, family or event. When a respective option is on, notes can be viewed on a pedigree image.
  • Multimedia content - any kind of photo, an audio record or video record, that you attached to person, family or event. Also you can add text or other types of documents.
  • Sources - a special object that stores information about archive or another types of documents or articles.
  • Groups - an object that can unite different individuals using some common attribute or relation. For example, you can create "The Great Patriotic War veterans" group and use it after, to quickly find all persons participated in WWII.

To access a list of records of each type, you use tabs.

The lists' columns show you the most important information.

You can use any column to order a list. To do that just click a column header.

The record summary area shows links to other related records as blue underlined text. If you click such text you jump to a respective record in this or another list.
