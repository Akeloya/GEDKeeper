Family record

Family record is one of the main record type available in a genealogical database. Family record stores information about family, conjoints, events and data from the family's life, children. You can also add text note, reference to source and multimedia object into a family record.

There is a table on the "Families" tab of the main working window, where you can find all families stored in database. Each family record has a summary area to the right of the table, where you can view entire information about family, conjoints, children, events and where you can jump to other records.

See also: Person, Event, Source, Note, Multimedia, Location, Address.
