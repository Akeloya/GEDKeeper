Tools: merging duplicates

This tool removes repetitions (duplicates) among persons in database. The tool shows a prompting window to you, where you must manually select (or give this choice to GEDKeeper) a record to remain and a record to delete. When removed person has links, they may be moved into remained person.

Merging duplicates tool also processes notes, families and sources.

It is recommended to search duplicates with "Check the year of birth" option turned on ("Options" tab). This reduces number of false operations.
