Correspondence

Correspondence records store information about letters, correspondence with other relatives, researchers and archives.

A correspondence record has subject field, correspondent, relation kind (visit, call, letter and so on), relation time. You can also add some text notes and multimedia content, for example, you can attach conversation recording or scanned letter image.

You can easily access entire correspondence using the united table on "Communication" tab of the main window. There is a record summary to the right of the table, where you can view detailed information and also view references to other records.
