Law

Everything below is specific to the Russian Federation.

Constitution of the Russian Federation, Clause 1 Article 23

Everyone shall have the right to privacy, personal and family secret, honor and reputation protection.

Constitution of the Russian Federation, Clause 1 Article 24

No one shall collect, store, use and spread privacy information about a person without the person's consent.

The Federal Law of the Russian Federation No 152-FL "About personal data", July 27, 2006
