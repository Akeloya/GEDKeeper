Tools: databases compare

There are two types of comparison available: internal comparison and compare with other database.

Internal comparison just searches possible duplicates of person records. There are some criteria to find similar or identical men. It is worth noting that this compare sometimes gives false positive, especially in cases of past centuries, when two or more children of single family had the same name. That is, GEDKeeper explicitly does not filter out brothers and sisters with the same name.

Database compare checks person records of two different databases. The comparison shows equal family names and men with equal full names from the both databases in dedicated lists. For your information and further analysis, the lists show birthday and day of death for those people. This compare is useful when you need to find perspective matching in different databases.
