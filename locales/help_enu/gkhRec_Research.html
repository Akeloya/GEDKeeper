Research record

You use research records to manage research main directions and priority of those directions. You create a research for some family branch and assign relative priority for this research, then you track a period of time when research is in progress, check the current research status and completion state.

You can split research up into individual tasks, add correspondence between and communication with relatives or other researchers using correspondence record, add social groups to easily find all people, that were researched under this search direction.

You add description of search progress and required comments using text notes.

There is a uniting table on the "Researches" tab of the main working window, which includes all researches available in database. Research summary to the right of the table shows all information about selected research.

See also: Task, Correspondence, Group, Note.
