Access management

There are three types of access restriction:

  • Confidentially -- confidential data never appears in reports, diagrams or exported content.
  • Locked -- no one can edit locked data without previous unlocking the data.
  • Private -- currently this access restriction is not implemented; future releases will encrypt any private data.

There are three modes of processing protected data:

  •  Maximum protection -- GEDKeeper does not show neither confidential nor private data.
  •  Medium protection -- all data is visible except private one.
  •  No protection -- GEDKeeper shows any data.

Modes of processing protected data affect three data view: tables of person and family records, tree diagrams and pedigrees. Maximum protection is on by default. You can change current protection mode by double clicking mode icon at bottom right corner of the main window.
